About Easton's Emporium

Our Goal!

Easton's Emporium was established with a single goal. That goal is to create a place for nerds to be nerds. Here on Easton's Emporium you can find the latest news on nerdy topics, purchase custom 3D models for tabletop games, find rulings for some of your favorite nerdy vices or even learn how to make your own tabletop game supplies using a 3D printer of your own!

Services we offer:

  • News Feed for Nerds!
  • Purchasable Custom Models!
  • Rulings and Regulations!
  • Info on 3D Printing!
  • Pointers on Painting!

We Take Requests!

If you have a nerdy game, hobby or pastime that we never mention please let us know! We are always looking to expand our horizons when it comes to nerdy topics. Easton's Emporium has someone just waiting to respond to you!

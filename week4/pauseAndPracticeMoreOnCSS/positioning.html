Oldschool D&D editions make modern comeback!

August 23rd, 2021 | Dungeons & Dragons

Despite the popularity of D&D 5th edition, it seems many players are returning to older editions.
The recent preference in the oldschool tabletop appears to focus primarily on the 3rd and 4th
editions. It would appear that most players prefer the complexity of the older versions of the game
as opposed to the more simplistic 5th edition.

Rare Black Lotus found in an unopened Alpha booster!

August 11th, 2021 | Magic the Gathering

John Johnson purchased a box of Magic the Gathering booster packs back in August of 1993 upon the
games release. Little did he know that fateful decision would lead to a small fortune twenty-eight years
later. Mr. Johnson opened most of the boosters back in 1993 but he purposfully left some unopened.
When he recently opened them Mr. Johnson found a Black Lotus worth upwards of $400,000.

Games Workshop removed Aeldari from their roster!

July 20th, 2021 | Warhammer 40k

Games Workshop, the company behind the largely successful tabletop game Warhammer 40k, recently
announced that they would be removing the Aeldari race from their army roster. This news comes as a
huge hit to fans who enjoyed playing their Aeldari army. Some fans have even threatened lawsuits for
compensation spent on the expensive Aeldari models prior to this decision by Games Workshop.

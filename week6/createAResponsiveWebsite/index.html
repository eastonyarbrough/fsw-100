Dungeon's & Dragons Rulings:

The Rogue Class!

As a rogue these are your starting stats:

Hit Dice: 1d8 per rogue level.

Hit Points: 1d8 + Constitution Modifier.

Proficiencies:

  • Light armor
  • Simple weapons
  • Hand crossbows
  • Longswords
  • Rapiers
  • Shortswords

Tools: Your choice of thieves tools.

Saving Throws: Dexterity and Intelligence.

Skills: Choose any four skills.

Choose your starting equipment:

Primary Weapon: Choose either (a) a rapier or (b) a shortsword.

Secondary Weapon: Choose either (a) shortbow w/ 20 arrows or (b) a shortsword.

Starting Pack: Choose either (a) Burglar's Pack, (b) Dungeoneer's Pack or (c) Explorer's Pack.

No special items.

The Wizard Class!

As a wizard these are your starting stats:

Hit Dice: 1d6 per wizard level.

Hit Points: 1d6 + Constitution Modifier.

Proficiencies:

  • Daggers
  • Darts
  • Slings
  • Quarterstaffs
  • Light Crossbows

Tools: None.

Saving Throws: Intelligence and Wisdom.

Skills: Choose any two skills.

Choose your starting equipment:

Primary Weapon: Choose either (a) a quarterstaff or (b) a dagger.

Secondary Weapon: Choose either (a) a component pouch or (b) an arcane focus.

Starting Pack: Choose either (a) Scholar's Pack or (b) Explorer's Pack.

You also get a spellbook.
